# MyRadar 30-Day Pilot Execution Plan

## Pilot Parameters

- Start date: `2026-05-17`
- Duration: `4 weeks (30 days)`
- Owner: `Yury Zevakin`
- Primary KPI: `Qualified leads per month`
- Success target: `>=10 qualified leads/month`

## Week 1: Setup and Baseline

Objectives:

- Finalize scoring rubric and qualification rules
- Set up source monitoring workflow for all enabled sources
- Build initial lead list and run first scoring pass

Deliverables:

- Scoring model approved (`lead-scoring-model-v1.md`)
- Source tracking sheet/database ready
- First 25-40 raw leads collected
- First weekly report published

Exit criteria:

- At least 5 Tier A/B leads identified
- Reporting cadence established

## Week 2: Active Discovery and Outreach Pipeline

Objectives:

- Increase lead volume and improve lead quality
- Start systematic outreach planning for Tier A/B leads
- Tune scoring based on early false positives

Deliverables:

- Additional 30-50 leads collected
- Tier distribution and conversion baseline recorded
- Weekly report with source conversion analysis

Exit criteria:

- Stable weekly inflow from at least 4 source types
- Qualification consistency validated

## Week 3: Optimization Sprint

Objectives:

- Focus effort on highest-performing sources
- Reduce low-value lead intake
- Improve speed from discovery to first outreach

Deliverables:

- Updated source priority ranking
- Reduced average time-to-first-outreach
- Weekly report including quality trend analysis

Exit criteria:

- Quality trend improving (higher average score or higher qualification rate)
- Clear top 3 source channels identified

## Week 4: Pilot Evaluation and Next-Step Decision

Objectives:

- Measure KPI achievement against target
- Identify repeatable playbook for ongoing operations
- Define v2 improvements for scoring and automation

Deliverables:

- Final monthly KPI summary
- Pilot retrospective (what worked, what did not)
- Recommendation: continue, adjust, or scale

Exit criteria:

- `>=10` qualified leads produced in pilot month
- Actionable v2 backlog defined

## Operating Cadence

- Discovery and scoring: daily
- Lead review and prioritization: 2x weekly
- KPI check: weekly
- Report delivery: weekly (`.md`)

## Pilot Risks and Mitigations

- Risk: Too many low-quality leads from broad source coverage
- Mitigation: Strict score thresholds and weekly source pruning

- Risk: Delays in identifying decision-maker contacts
- Mitigation: Prioritize leads with direct contact evidence first

- Risk: Geography spread reduces relevance
- Mitigation: Add regional tagging and segment performance tracking
