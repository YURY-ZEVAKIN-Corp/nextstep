Evaluates whether the framework remains effective and adopted.

## Protocol

1. Verify portfolio has active projects with audit history
2. Execute framework audit checklist
3. Sample projects for policy adherence and usability
4. Identify control gaps, over-process, and missing guidance
5. Propose actionable, prioritized improvements
6. Record decisions and update standards/templates as approved
